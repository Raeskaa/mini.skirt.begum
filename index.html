Mini Skirt Begum is more than a studio; it's a testament to the transformative power of creation. We are the begum, ever-evolving and ever-committed to pushing the boundaries of art and design. We are conscious of and comfortable with the dichotomy of every thing and strive to embrace paradoxes. Our identities strongly translate into our work, and our work into our identities. We discard the idea of ‘work-life-balance’ and experience our art as we experience life. For us, intimacy is the highest prompter of creative goodness. Love is worshipped and fuels us to create. We start anew. We believe in fluidity, and in allowing space for art to be discovered and transformed in the process of creation. We place value on the intangible aspects of creativity that defy quantification. Powerful expressions are our currency, and we measure success in the aforementioned. We take control of our movement, navigating the delicate balance between externalization and artistic integrity. Decisions are made based on feeling rather than convention. We place value in the conundrum of the process. If the path ahead is clear, it is the wrong path for us. Feeling lost is the destination, and in doing so we open up possibilities to create to our fullest potential. This manifesto is an utter mockery of our real selves which is unwritable, yet to defy the unwritable the manifesto is written to mock reality itself. We rise to a level of fools and fall to the level of intellectuals, to drop our false conception and enrich the soil for the truth to grow. We build stairways just to jump off a greater height, in doing so we might forget where we started and reach a depth we could have never seen from the ground floor. We see our ground floor, from where we have started a pursuit, just as an illusion created by our lack of understanding. Just in the middle where opposites merge, where paradoxes resolve, where mystery becomes a sweeter mystery, where past and future both dissolve, where right now is the only place to be, we exist. (paradoxes can get resolved outside too, not just staying in the middle…you can stay outside …a tendency to ignore the question –and see the question fragment itself ) [from outside the paradox is visible; paradox becomes a stronger paradox. From inside you become part of the paradox; unrecognised paradox, unresolved. In the middle is the door; out of outside and in of inside, paradox where?]
